# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project expects respectful, direct, and professional collaboration.

- Be constructive in disagreement.
- Critique code, behavior, and decisions, not people.
- Assume good intent, but be clear about impact when something is harmful.
- Respect different levels of experience and backgrounds.
- Do not harass, insult, threaten, discriminate against, or exclude others.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- personal attacks
- abusive or insulting language
- sexualized language or attention
- intimidation or harassment
- doxxing or sharing private information without permission
- repeated bad-faith disruption of discussions or reviews

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the maintainers through the security contact listed in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, pull requests, issues, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent removal from project spaces.
